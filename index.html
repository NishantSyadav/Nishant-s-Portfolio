Works
UniBridge is a student-focused discussion and mentorship platform built especially for engineering students. It connects juniors and seniors in one space to ask doubts, share knowledge, and guide each other academically and professionally. Students can post questions, participate in discussions, and access useful learning resources. A dedicated events section allows different colleges to post seminars, hackathons, workshops, and technical events so students never miss important opportunities. UniBridge aims to reduce the communication gap between semesters and institutions. It also supports preparation for placements, coding contests, and technical interviews through peer learning. By creating a collaborative academic ecosystem, UniBridge encourages growth, networking, and continuous improvement.
